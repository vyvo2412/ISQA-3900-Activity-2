Food Places

I grew up in Vietnam so ramen and noodle soup are always my favorite types of food. A bowl of ramen with the broth freshly cooked after many hours will warm up your body in cold days. I can eat ramen and noodle soups any time in the day. There are many types of ramen and soups that I'll never have enough.

  • JINYA Ramen Bar
  • IPHO#1 The Noodle House
  • Ika Ramen
  • The Banh Mi Shop

I also love boba with passion. It's originally a milk tea drink with tapioca bubbles. However, throughout time, there are many new types of boba that add more flavors to this type of drink. Beside milk tea, there are slush, punch, classic tea, fruity drinks, etc. Here's are my two favorite boba places in town.

  • Kungfu Tea
  • Thristea

Exploring Nature

I used to hate walking or any types of exercises. After a few times going on hikes, I started to appreciate nature more. Now I love hiking and spending time outside. Here are my go-to places.

  • Hitchcock Nature Center
  • Standing Bear Lake
  • Zorinsky Lake Park
