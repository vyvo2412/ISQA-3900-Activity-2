Food Places

I grew up in Vietnam so ramen and noodle soup are always my favorites. A bowl of ramen with the broth freshly cooked after many hours will warm up your body in cold days. I can eat ramen and noodle soups any time in the day. There are many types of ramen and soups that I'll never have enough.

  • JINYA Ramen Bar
  • IPHO#1 The Noodle House
  • Ika Ramen
  • The Banh Mi Shop

I love boba with passion. It's originally a milk tea drink with tapioca bubbles. However, throughout time, there are many new types of boba that make this drink so flavorable. Beside milk tea, there are slush, punch, classic tea, fruity drinks, etc.
